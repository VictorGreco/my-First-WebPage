ABOUT NYSL
Mission
To support young athlets living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.
Vision
The Nothside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindul athletes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leathership.
General Information
The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's nothside neighborhoods an environment in wich to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full.time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league member.
